Avondale Park
Avondale Park is a place holding a lot of great history behind it from the time the land was purchased in 1888. From a natural underground spring, baseball fields, the amphitheater, the pond, rose garden, and its Villa, there are a multitude of reasons to visit Avondale Park! While the park had begun to feel the wear of time a few years back, the Friends of Avondale Park group was form and renovations have been underway and much completed to help the upkeep of the park. The park has since become a beautiful as well as historical landmark of the Birmingham area. Come visit Avondale Park today!
