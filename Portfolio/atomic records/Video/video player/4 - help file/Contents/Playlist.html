Playlist Overview

Node name Description
videoPath Video file, the direct path of the video file.
thumbPath Thumbnail, the direct path of the thumbnail.
imagePath Preview image, the direct path of the preview image.
title The title of the video.
desc The description of the video.

Note: Theres no limits to how many entries you can have in the playlist, you can have as little as 1 or as many as 200 thats entirely up to you.
