Overview

This player is designed to blend in perfectly with any website. It's interface is both Elegant and Stylish.

The unique feature of this file is the slideshow thumbs, the controls are visible in the top right-hand corner of the screen, to start the slideshow simply click on the play icon, or alterntivetly you can also click on the enter key on your keyboard. The slideshow has a timer that resets the position of the thumbs if the user is over it before skipping to the next row.
